Who We Are:

Copy Validator is a global verification service platform that aims to solve the problem of ensuring the authenticity of certified true copies of documents. Our solution involves adding a Bona Fide stamp and serial number on certified copies, thus adding some legal weight and an extra layer of True ownership.

Here are a few things to know and you can do to get started and protect your Bona Fide Documents:

Document Submission:

Steps to submit your Document for Verification:

  1. Ensure your Document(s) are Certified as True Copies of the Original Documents by a registered and Licensed Advocate/Lawyer/Commissioner of Oaths or Accredited Practicing Notary Public
  2. Fill the form with accurate information
  3. Ensure your File has an Identification Number. This Number can vary depending on the type of Document. It can be in the form of a Reference Number or Certificate Number. In case of a recommendation Letter without a reference Number, kindly enter your Account Username followed by a Number for each document. John1 for the First Document, John2 for the Second Document Submission and so on.
  4. Upload Your Document.

Note: Each individual upload shall be accompanied with its Individual form

Document Validation:

Our team will carefully Examine and validate your documents to ensure accuracy and authenticity through a due Diligence operation and Back Ground Checks. Therefore ensure that the Contact Details on the submission form are correct and reachable to avoid rejection of your Documents.

Download Validated Documents:

Once your documents have been validated/Verified, you'll receive a notification, then you and your Document users( Any One with access to your document ID or Serial Number) can Search/Verify and download the file securely on the platform.

Your Documents shall be available for as long as you and your document users need them.
